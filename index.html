Jiggle

Jiggle is a high-protein chocolate bar enriched with mealworm powder

Jiggle offers a fun and sustainable way to taste the future of food.

Packed with complete proteins, Vitamin B12 and prebiotics, Jiggle gives you a lot more than the average protein bar.

For centuries, people around the world have eaten insects as part of their normal diet. One of the most promising species is the mealworm. Mealworms are packed with important nutrients and protein, and they're much more environmentally sustainable than meat.
